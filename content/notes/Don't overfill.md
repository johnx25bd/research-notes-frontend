I'm finding as I age that overfilled things are often a source of problems. 

Overfilled bags. Overfilled cupboards. Overfilled schedules. Overfilled cars. 

There's a healthy capacity, and as that capacity gets exceeded, things fall apart. 

Interestingly, underfilled things also tend to break down over time ...
