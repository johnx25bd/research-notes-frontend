Presented with Taylor Oshan at FOSS4G North America 2025. The talk walks through Astral as an open-source framework for working with geospatial data in decentralized systems: the Location Protocol for digitally signed spatial records, a modular library of location-proof strategies, and Spatial.sol utilities for smart contracts.
